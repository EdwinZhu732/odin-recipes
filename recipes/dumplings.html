Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are pieces of cooked dough, often wrapped around a filling. The filling can be a variety of things.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Stew or soup, for cooking and serving

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Add milk and mix. Let it rest for a few minutes
  3. Drop batter in soup and cook
  4. Serve the dumplings and soup!
