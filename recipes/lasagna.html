Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta made of wide and flat sheets. It usually includes sauce and cheese. Making lasagna can take a while, but the results are well worth it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomata products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
