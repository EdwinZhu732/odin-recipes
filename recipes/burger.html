Burger

Description

A burger is a sanwich consisting of fillings, usually ground beef, in between buns.

Ingredients

  • Large egg
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Ground beef
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat grill and lightly oil grate
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  3. Add beef and bread crumbs and mix with hands or fork
  4. Form into patties
  5. Place patties on grill
  6. Serve and enjoy!
